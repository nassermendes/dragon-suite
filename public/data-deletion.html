Dragon Suite - Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: February 12, 2025

How to Request Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your personal data through any of these methods:

1. Email Request

Send an email to nassermendes@gmail.com with:

  • Subject line: "Data Deletion Request - Dragon Suite"
  • Your full name
  • Email address associated with your account
  • List of connected social media accounts

2. In-App Deletion

Within the Dragon Suite app:

  1. Go to Settings
  2. Select "Privacy & Data"
  3. Click "Delete My Data"
  4. Confirm your choice

What We Delete

  • Your account information
  • Social media connections and tokens
  • Content drafts and schedules
  • Analytics data
  • Usage history

Deletion Timeframe

We will process your deletion request within 30 days. You will receive an email confirmation once the deletion is complete.

Need Help?
