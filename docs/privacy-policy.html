Dragon Suite - Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 11, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Dragon Suite collects the following information:

  • Authentication tokens for social media platforms
  • Basic profile information from connected social media accounts
  • Content you choose to upload through our app
  • App usage data and analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Post content to your social media accounts as authorized
  • Improve and optimize our app
  • Communicate with you about our services

3. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are encrypted and stored securely.

4. Third-Party Services

Dragon Suite integrates with third-party services (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). When you connect these services, their privacy policies will also apply.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. We will notify you of any material changes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at privacy@dragonsuite.app
